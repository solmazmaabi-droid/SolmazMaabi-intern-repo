## üîç Research & Learn

### Professional vs. Unprofessional Behaviour in a Casual Work Environment

**Professional behaviour includes:**
- Communicating clearly and respectfully in messages and meetings  
- Being punctual and prepared for meetings  
- Listening actively and allowing others to speak  
- Following through on commitments  
- Giving constructive feedback  

**Unprofessional behaviour includes:**
- Interrupting others or dominating conversations  
- Using disrespectful or aggressive language  
- Ignoring messages or deadlines without explanation  
- Making jokes that could be offensive or exclusionary  
- Responding defensively to feedback  

A casual environment still requires respect and accountability.

---

### Respectful Communication in Remote Teams
Respectful communication in remote teams includes:
- Using a clear, polite, and professional tone in written messages  
- Avoiding sarcasm or ambiguous language that could be misinterpreted  
- Acknowledging messages, even if a full response will come later  
- Handling disagreements calmly and privately  
- Being mindful of time zones and availability  

Clear communication helps prevent misunderstandings in remote work.

---

### Giving and Receiving Feedback Professionally
Professional feedback should:
- Focus on actions or outcomes, not personal traits  
- Be specific and constructive  
- Assume good intent  
- Be delivered respectfully and at the right time  

When receiving feedback, it is important to listen, ask clarifying questions, and avoid reacting defensively. Feedback should be viewed as an opportunity to learn and improve.

---

### Working with Supervisors
Effective collaboration with supervisors involves:
- Preparing questions or updates before meetings  
- Communicating progress and challenges clearly  
- Asking for clarification when expectations are unclear  
- Following up on agreed actions  

This helps build trust and ensures alignment.
